Chris Hadfield

A Canadian Hero

Astronaut Chris Hadfield

Here is a Timeline with some of Chris's accomplishments

  • Born - 1959 Sarnia, Ontario
  • 1977 - Graduated high school as Ontario Scholar
  • 1982 - Bachelor's Degree Mechanical Engineering, Royal Military College
  • 1983 - Top graduate of Basic Jet Training from CFB Moose Jaw
  • 1988 - Top Pilot Graduate of the USAF Test pilot school
  • 1991 - US Navy test pilot of the year
  • 1992 - Master of Science in Aviation Systems
  • 1996 - Honorary Doctorate of engineering from the Royal Military College
  • 1996 - Member of the Order of Ontario
  • 1999 - Honorary Doctorate of Law from Trent University
  • 2001 - Vanier Award
  • 2001 - Meritorious Service Cross
  • 2002 - NASA Exceptional Service Medal
  • 2003 - Queen's Golden Jubilee Medal
